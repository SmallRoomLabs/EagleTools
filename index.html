Eagle Layout Tools

This site generates Eagle commands for aligning parts in circles, arcs and rectangular arrays. If can also draw text among a circle or build a circle out of many short lengths of top/bottom copper.

The generated commands can be copied and pasted into the command line in the Eagle layout window.
